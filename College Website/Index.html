Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering


In today's digital scenario, Electronics and Communication Engineering is a consistently developing industry. Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) involves research work, developing, designing, and testing of modern electronic gadgets used in different systems in day to day life . Ever since the advancement of innovation, Electronics and Communication has become an essential discipline that is important by every aspect. Electronics and communication engineering, using sophisticated hardware and relevant technologies, have been able to develop almost error free high speed communication links that have led to the current era being dubbed the “Information Age”. Students of Electronics and communication engineering can make their careers in different fields like manufacturing industry, telecommunication & networking, automotive and IT industry in government and private also. Many opportunities are always coming for ECE due to integration of electronics into various industry verticals. We at GMIT hope to produce skilled professionals who can carry on with this heritage. With our excellent infrastructure and dedicated faculty, we do believe we’ll succeed.



Department of Computer Science & Engineering


True to its commitment to the core principle of Industry Linked, GMIT has recognized the urgent need of the industry for next generation digitally skilled engineers. Responding to this need, it has upgraded its curriculum to incorporate Digital Transformation Technologies – a pool of digital technologies that will transform the way organizations are run. According to a recent release by NASSCOM, the skills of the future would be Big Data Analytics, Cloud & Cybersecurity Services, IoT, Artificial Intelligence and many other Digital Technologies. A huge demand is foreseen for roles such as mobile app development, social media, data scientists & platform engineering. The B.Tech in CSE with emphasis on Digital Transformation Technologies has received instant endorsement by industry leaders. With industry backing of the B.Tech CSE curriculum with emphasis on Digital Transformation Technologies, B.Tech CSE students can look forward to take on leadership roles in digitally transforming organizations that is the need of the future.
